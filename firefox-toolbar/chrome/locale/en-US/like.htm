Facebook Toolbar for Firefox

By downloading and installing this add-on, you acknowledge and have reviewed our Privacy Policy and agree to our Terms.

While the add-on is installed and the Like feature enabled, it will display the total number of likes for the page you are viewing. In order to provide a personalized experience, Facebook will see some technical information such as the URL of the page you are on, your IP address and the date and time you visited the site. All information collected by you simply browsing the web while this add-on is installed will only be used in anonymous or aggregate form and will not identify you. If you want to like something using this add-on's Like button, you'll have to log into your Facebook account and as with other likes they'll be posted to your profile.
